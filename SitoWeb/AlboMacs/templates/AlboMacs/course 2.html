About the Course

Innovative program aimed at training a new leading class at the cutting edge of today’s digital revolution, answering specific demands from national and international job markets. MaCS combines strong quantitative skills in computer and data science with the rigorous thinking of economics and management, blending traditional courses in economics, entrepreneurship, law and statistics with a significant presence of STEM topics, and training students on algorithms, coding, artificial intelligence, data management. Graduates will be fully bilingual in digital and business. The high percentage of foreign instructors and students and the opportunity to spend the third year abroad create a multicultural experience.
